# Prompt Iteration

## Initial Prompt
Write a reply to the following customer message.

---

## Revision 1
Write a polite and professional customer support reply. Acknowledge the issue and suggest next steps. Do not invent policies.

---

## Revision 2
Write a polite and professional customer support reply in under 120 words. Acknowledge the issue, ask for missing information if necessary, and suggest next steps. Do not invent policies. Escalate legal or refund disputes to a human agent.

---

## What changed
Revision 1 added professionalism and reduced hallucination.
Revision 2 added safety rules and escalation guidance.

---

## What improved
The responses became clearer, more professional, and less likely to invent company policies.
